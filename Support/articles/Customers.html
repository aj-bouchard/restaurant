Seating

Number of Customers Popup:
Follow these steps to enable a window to select the number of customers for a table each time an order is made:

1. While in Manager, click Settings and then scroll down to App Settings
2. Click the checkbox next to "Number of customers popup"
3. Press "Save" at the bottom of the screen.

Assign an order to a Customer without a table
By enabling this option, servers will now have the ability to assign an order to a customer's name instead of having to select a specific table. This is useful for creating temporary customer tabs and managing variable seating arrangements:

1. From the iPad Home Screen, open the Settings app.
2. Scroll down and select Restaurant from the list of apps on the left side of that window.
3. From the resulting menu on the right, scroll down to the very bottom and choose "Developer"
4. Scroll down to the Functionality Settings and toggle the setting named "Enable the overview panel on the floor view".

A white arrow pointing to the right should now appear on the left edge of the screen in the Lightspeed Restaurant POS app
